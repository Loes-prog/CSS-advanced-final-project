How does AI work?

AI works by using data to mimic human intelligence, enabling machines to perform tasks that typically require human input. It processes vast amounts of information, learns from patterns, and makes decisions or predictions based on that data. Whether it’s analyzing trends, generating content, or automating repetitive tasks, AI helps improve efficiency and accuracy. Over time, it continues to learn and adapt, becoming smarter and more effective. It’s not magic—it’s powerful technology making our lives easier and businesses more efficient!
